## **Associated VMs**

The **Associated VMs** tab lists all running VMs instances connected to the isolated network.

### **Conclusion**

The Associated VMs tab provides a clear overview of all VM instances connected to the isolated network, making it easier to monitor, organize, and manage active resources efficiently.
